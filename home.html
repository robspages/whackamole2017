Rules

Judging

To be a judge, you will need to:
  • Not have a submission - judges are not eligible for the contest
  • Be an employee of One Door
  • Be present in the office on April 27th during the judging

Judging will be done in secret, in a private Slack channel for the purpose. All judges may weigh in on any/all of the Scoring categories - the final score in each category will be the average of the provided scores.

Scoring

You will be judged on:
  • Difficulty: How many features did you add and are they all working? Up to 20 points
  • Execution: How well can our panel of judges understand and work with your code? Up to 30 points
  • UI Usability: Can users figure out how to play without lots of instructions and/or hassle? Up to 30 points
  • UI Aesthetics: Is it pleasing to see and use? Up to 20 points
